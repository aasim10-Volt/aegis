Please read carefully before continuing
AEGIS is a transparent system — everything it monitors, you are told about now. Your workspace activity supports fair contribution tracking, protects you from carrying others' work, and gives lecturers accurate information for grade disputes.
Workspace activity monitoring
Google Drive edits, file revisions, and document activity are polled every 72 hours. Only metadata is recorded — document contents are never read.
Contribution scoring
A contribution score is computed from task completion and workspace engagement, visible to you and your lecturer. It feeds ghosting detection and workload balancing.
What lecturers can see
Lecturers see team health scores and flagged anomalies — not raw logs. No automated decision affects your standing without a faculty review first.
Your rights under PDPA
You may view your own data, appeal any placement, and withdraw from optional collection at any time. Data is retained for one academic year then deleted.
